We offer a wide range of classes, from building simple creations with LEGO to programming using block coding. Our curriculum incorporates diverse cultural philosophies, teaching creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving through unique global perspectives!

They learn about flow programming, advanced attachments, optimization, and much more, including designing and building Japenese humanoids to aid in global crises, such as disaster relief and healthcare support.

Students explore Spike Prime LEGO Mindstorms and learn how to build and program a sturdy robot.
