Learn More About Our Work

OwnABot Academy is an educational institution specializing in robotics, offering programs for various age groups, from kindergarten to middle school. The academy stands out by incorporating cultural elements from around the world into its teaching methods, blending traditional engineering philosophies with modern technology. Students explore tools like LEGO WeDo, EV3 Mindstorms, Spike Prime LEGO Mindstorms, and Python programming while learning global approaches to creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration, fostering a well-rounded perspective on robotics and innovation.

The work ethic of OwnABot Academy is rooted in a strong commitment to STEM education, inclusivity, and hands-on learning, with a unique focus on incorporating diverse cultural techniques and philosophies to inspire creativity in robotics and engineering.
