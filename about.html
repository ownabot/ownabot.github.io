Learn More About Our Work

OwnABot Academy is an educational institution focused on STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education, specifically emphasizing robotics. The academy offers various programs for different age groups, starting from kindergarten to middle school. Their curriculum includes classes on LEGO Wedo, EV3 Mindstorms, Spike Prime Lego Mindstorms, and even Python programming.

The work ethic of OwnABot Academy is characterized by a strong commitment to STEM education, inclusivity, hands-on learning, and a passion for robotics and engineering.
