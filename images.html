Beginner Robotics Program

At OwnABot, we hold a introductory robotics program, explaining how to build and program in Wedo. In this class, students learn how to build various contraptions like a windmill, a crocodile, and a swing using only lego technic pieces. See how our class unfolds!!!!

Intermediate Robotics Program

Once students grasp the basic concepts, they move on more complicated softwares like EV3 Mindstrom and Spike Prime In our Innovative Robotics Engineer curriculum, we explain how to build a fully functioning robot, from the legs of it to its brain. We also show to program these robots to complete multiple tasks using the EV3 Mindstorms system.

STEAM Program

In addition to our robotics curriculum, OwnABot holds a variety of STEAM related events to introduce students to topics like Aerodynamics, Architecture, Programming, and much more. Here are photos showing how amazing this program works!!!
