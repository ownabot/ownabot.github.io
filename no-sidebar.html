Our Team

Our team has been working hard for the last 4 years to bring you the best lego robotics curriculum. We have worked with over 500 students across multiple locations in the Bay Area and can teach anything from the basics to advanced lego robotics.

Advait Marathe

Advait has had 6 years of robotics experience and has taught for 4 of those years. He is currently a student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Business Administration. He is experienced in teaching having taught for the last 4 years and is the creator of the curriculum we use here at OwnABot Academy. Finally, Advait has also written his on book about how you can get started with lego robotics called "Have Fun Building and Programming Robots" which you can find at www.tinyurl.com/roboticsbook .

Shital Savarkar

Shital has been in the tech industry for the last 15 years specifically in the biotech industry. She first started teaching robotics back in 2015 and has continued to help teach Lego Wedo, and Lego Mindstorms. In the past she has had extensive teaching experience, teaching at Language schools, and tutoring others.

Upendra Marathe

Upendra Marathe has been teaching since 2015 and is currently a Program Manager at Nvidia. He has been teaching for the last 4 years, and has helped over 100 students learn about robotics. He has also coached multiple FLL and FTC teams and can help others coach and create their own teams.
